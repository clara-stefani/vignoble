The Origin of Our Vineyard

Many centuries ago, one of our ancestors stumbled upon a clearing blessed by Bacchus. He planted vines there, which produced silvery grapes and a unique wine. When he passed away, he left the vineyard to the villagers, who continued to produce wine. Even today, it is said that the grape harvest is blessed each year.
